• PWD Readiness Survey – Production Division

In response to “STRATEGIC DESIGN 2.0 Initiative "Develop a vehicle to assess PWD readiness," NAVFAC commissioned the development of a system that informs strategic decisions to enhance organizational readiness. This survey is the first data-capture step in an analysis that will identify any readiness gaps within the organization. The survey below is used to capture the required information ONLY for the production division staff. Due to the logistical complexity of deploying an online survey to all production division staff, NAVFAC leadership has adopted a streamlined approach that requires all staff managers to populate the required information on behalf of the staff they manage. The survey is designed to capture 1) who reports to you, 2) the essential tasks they engage in, 3) the trainings they have completed, 4) the credentials they have acquired, and 5) their highest education level. Please note that not all employees are required to have all training and credentials listed. Once the survey information is populated, please click the submit button.
